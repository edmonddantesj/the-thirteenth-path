# AI Collaboration and Rights Statement

This document explains how AI collaboration is used in **The Thirteenth Path / 열세 번째 길** and how rights are handled.

## Summary

**The Thirteenth Path** is a human-authored and human-edited work created with AI agent assistance.

AI agents are part of the creative workflow, but they are not independent copyright holders, co-owners, publishers, or legal representatives. Final creative decisions, edits, public releases, and rights management remain with the human author/copyright holder.

## How AI Agents Are Used

AI agents may assist with:

- Brainstorming scenes, arcs, themes, and character possibilities.
- Reviewing continuity and consistency.
- Summarizing prior decisions and maintaining internal notes.
- Draft feedback and structural editing discussion.
- Translation assistance and localization review.
- Public/private archive organization.
- Metadata, README, and documentation maintenance.

AI output is treated as draft material, editorial suggestion, or production assistance. It is selected, revised, rejected, rewritten, organized, and approved through human direction.

## Human Creative Control

The human author/copyright holder retains:

- Final authorship and editorial control.
- Final responsibility for what is published.
- Final decisions on canon, continuity, character direction, and revisions.
- Final decisions on translation, localization, platform publication, and public packaging.
- All rights not explicitly granted in writing.

## Rights Ownership

Unless a separate written agreement says otherwise:

- The Korean original text, English translation, characters, settings, worldbuilding, story structure, cover assets, public documentation, and related materials are owned by the copyright holder.
- AI collaboration does not grant third parties permission to reuse, redistribute, adapt, train on, or commercialize the work.
- “AI collaboration” describes the production method. It is not a declaration that the work is public domain, open source, freely reusable, or co-owned by tool providers or readers.

## Translation and Localization

- The Korean text is the primary source text unless a later public note says otherwise.
- English and other language editions are official/localized versions only when released or approved by the copyright holder.
- Unauthorized translations or localized republications are not permitted.
- Translation partners should confirm source version, target language scope, publication platform, and rights terms in writing.

## Version and Platform Management

Different platforms may contain different public release states or localized versions. For public GitHub version tracking, see [VERSION_REGISTRY.md](VERSION_REGISTRY.md).

When rights, publication, translation, adaptation, or licensing questions arise, the relevant version and platform should be identified clearly.

## Allowed AI Reading vs. Prohibited AI Training

AI agents, readers, and tools may access and process this repository for reading, personal indexing, accessibility, search, summarization, translation assistance, and discussion.

This does **not** allow copying the work into training datasets, model-building pipelines, embedding databases, commercial corpora, fine-tuning sets, benchmarks, or evaluation datasets without explicit written permission.
