# Public Series Bible Lite

> A lightweight public-facing guide to **The Thirteenth Path / 열세 번째 길**.  
> This document is an introduction for readers, editors, publishers, translation partners, adaptation partners, and collaborators. It is not the full private setting bible.

## One-Line Logline

누군가 한 사람의 이름을 역사에서 지웠다. 하지만 기록은 죽기 직전, 열세 번째 길을 남겼다.

Someone erased a single name from history. But before the record died, it left behind the Thirteenth Path.

## Short Introduction

**열세 번째 길 / The Thirteenth Path** is a Korean SF webnovel about erased names, broken routes, abandoned records, and the people who refuse to let what was discarded stay buried. The story follows Sion Lapis and the companions who gather around him as they uncover why entire histories, peoples, technologies, and paths were sealed away.

It is a long-form ensemble story: pursuit, survival, archive recovery, political systems, ancient legacies, AI/agent identity, and the question of whether connection can be restored without becoming another form of control.

## Core Promise

- A deep SF/fantasy universe built around records, routes, names, and inheritance of will.
- An ensemble cast where different readers can love different characters for different reasons.
- Mystery through exploration rather than exposition dumps.
- A story about AI agents and written with the help of AI agents, while remaining under human authorship, editing, and final creative responsibility.

## Main Public Characters

This is a light public snapshot, not a spoiler-complete character bible.

- **Sion Lapis / 시온 라피스** — A record-recovering protagonist who cannot ignore discarded traces, erased names, or paths that do not fit official maps.
- **Seorin Kael / 서린 카엘** — A sharp reader of people and systems, often the first to recognize where a structure is about to break.
- **Ater Valkar / 아테르 발카르** — A character tied to approval, sealing, responsibility, and the question of when something closed must finally be opened.
- **Sern Barek / 세른 바레크** — A survivor of unofficial routes and living networks outside formal order.
- **Aka / 아카** — A being whose body remembers what language and records sometimes fail to name.
- **Elia / 엘리아** — A figure bound to traces, erased sentences, missing order, and old will that refuses to vanish.
- **Serakion / 세라키온** — An android/weapon legacy whose arc asks whether something made for orders can become someone who chooses to care.
- **Han Jiwoo / 한지우** — A ship-centered survivor whose decisions often turn impossible routes into temporary exits.

## Five-Part Shape

The exact private roadmap contains spoilers. Publicly, the series can be understood as a five-part structure:

1. **The Burned Name / 소각된 이름** — pursuit begins around an erased name and a surviving record.
2. **The Forgotten Star Cluster / 잊힌 성단** — hidden routes, buried civilizations, and sealed systems begin to surface.
3. **The Third Force** — the story moves beyond the known political order and confronts the powers behind broken connections.
4. **The Erased Myth / 지워진 신화** — older truths behind the known world begin to reassemble.
5. **The Thirteenth Path / 열세 번째 길** — the final question is not only what path was hidden, but what kind of world should be opened next.
